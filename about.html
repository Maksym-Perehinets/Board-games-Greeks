About us

Welcome to Geak Game, where board game enthusiasts come together to experience the joy of strategic play. Since our inception in 2023, Geak Game has been dedicated to curating an exceptional board games club that elevates the gaming experience for all. Located at Squer of Chervonoi Kalina Avenue, 36, in the heart of Lviv, our headquarters serve as a haven for those who share a passion for tabletop adventures.

Geak Game has evolved into a dynamic force within the board games club community, driven by a commitment to excellence and a love for the art of gaming. From classic favorites to modern masterpieces, our diverse collection caters to all tastes and preferences. Our team of passionate individuals, dispersed globally but unified in purpose, collaborates seamlessly to bring forth innovative events and experiences that transcend the ordinary.
